Veggie casserole

Description

Jamie olivers veggie casserole. A casserole with lots of veggies which works as great comfort food in any season.

I do my own version where I remove some ingredients like onions, mushrooms and vinegar & I add tomato paste for added acidity.

ingredients

  • Butternut squash
  • Tomato paste
  • Olive oil
  • Tapenade
  • Chickpeas
  • Can of crushed tomatoes
  • Sea salt
  1. Slice the butternut squash in half, remove the seeds & put it on the pan
  2. Put the oven on 175C & put the butternut squash in for 1 hour 50 minutes.
  3. Take a large pot & drizzle olive oil in there until it covers the bottom
  4. Put the tomato paste in the pan and have it on medium heat for 5-10minutes
  5. put 2 cans of crushed tomatoes in, put water in the pot equal to 1 can
  6. Add in the Tapenade, Salt and Chickpeas and let it simmer for 20 minutes then take it off the heat
  7. When the butternut squash is done, take the flesh with a spoon and put it in the pot and mix it around

    8. And now you got a beautiful, tasty veggie casserole!
